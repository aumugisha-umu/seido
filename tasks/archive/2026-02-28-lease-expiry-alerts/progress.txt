## Key Learnings
1. Belgian lease law thresholds: courte durée (≤3 ans) = 90j legal notice; 9 ans/longue durée = 180j
2. Alert tiers at 2×deadline / deadline+1month / deadline itself
3. JSONB metadata pattern avoids DB migration for new decision tracking
4. Client-safe utility extraction: pure functions in lib/utils/, server service re-exports
5. Tab styling: isAlert parameter pattern for orange alert tabs (same as interventions)
