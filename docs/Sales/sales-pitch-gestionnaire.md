> **Persona de reference**: Julien Vandenberghe, 42 ans, 280 logements, 150-300 interventions/mois

## 1. Sales Pitch (Verbal — Live Demo/Meeting)

### Version A — "You know this story" (Recommandee, ~70 sec)

> If you manage properties, you know this story.
>
> Tenant WhatsApps you Friday evening. You plan to reply Monday — but forget. Wednesday, they call, frustrated.
>
> Now the scramble starts. Find the right provider. Exchange messages, photos, quotes — across three different channels. Share the tenant's number and... you're blind. No visibility until someone calls to complain.
>
> When it's finally done, the invoice is in your email, the photos on WhatsApp, the quote in a PDF somewhere. And if a colleague needs to take over? They start from zero.
>
> That's what SEIDO eliminates.
>
> One platform where tenants, providers, and your team work together on each intervention. Every message, document, and status — in one place. You only get notified when your input is actually needed.
>
> What used to take 2 hours of follow-up takes 10 minutes. Your phone stops ringing for status updates. And anyone on your team can pick up any file with full context.
>
> You stop firefighting. You start managing.

### Version B — "2 hours a day" (Direct, ~55 sec)

> Property managers lose 2 hours a day to information chaos.
>
> Tenant texts on WhatsApp. Provider sends quotes by email. Photos are on someone's phone. Invoices in a folder somewhere. And when a colleague takes over a file — they start from zero.
>
> SEIDO puts all of that in one place.
>
> One platform where tenants report issues, providers submit quotes, and your whole team tracks progress — on the same intervention file. You only get notified when YOUR action is needed.
>
> Result: 10 minutes instead of 2 hours. 70% fewer status calls. And a team that can finally take over from each other seamlessly.
>
> SEIDO — manage with clarity, not chaos.

### Comparaison des versions

| Critere | Version A | Version B |
|---------|-----------|-----------|
| **Engagement** | Scenario fait hocher la tete — "c'est moi" | Plus froid, stat d'ouverture |
| **Rythme oral** | Phrases courtes, pauses naturelles | Legerement plus dense |
| **Memorabilite** | "Friday evening -> Wednesday call" = moment concret | "2h/day" = chiffre mais abstrait |
| **Closer** | Identity shift: "You stop firefighting" | Tagline: "Clarity, not chaos" |
| **Duree** | ~70 sec (parfait pour intro demo) | ~55 sec (pitch rapide) |
| **Meilleur pour** | Meeting, demo live, networking | Elevator pitch, cold intro |
